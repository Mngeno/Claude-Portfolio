I am a results-driven Geospatial Engineer with a Bachelor of Engineering in Geospatial Engineering (Second Class Honours) from the Technical University of Kenya, and over six years of progressive experience in GIS, surveying, land administration, and spatial data infrastructure.

Currently serving as a Senior GIS Specialist at Digireg Kenya Limited, I lead the design and implementation of county-scale Land Information Management Systems, oversee GIS data quality control, develop FME-driven ETL pipelines for large-scale data migration, and coordinate with national and county government stakeholders on land administration and spatial planning projects.

My work spans the full geospatial value chain — from GNSS/RTK field surveys and cadastral data capture to enterprise GIS database design, system training, and stakeholder engagement. Projects have covered Nakuru, Lamu, Kitui, Siaya, and Kilifi counties.
